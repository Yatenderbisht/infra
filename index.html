Makeup Artist - Mily Kalra

Mily Kalra is an MBA graduate from a top B-school however her love for makeup made her choose a career as a professional makeup artist over a corporate job. Over the years, Mily has excelled in her art by attending training from renowned and international makeup artists.

Mily has made a great name in the industry as one of the top bridal makeup artist in Gurgaon. Many of her brides choose her for her natural yet glam bridal makeup looks. Her clients consider her to be a great friend and an amazing artist at the same time.

She believes “if you can’t connect with a client mentally and emotionally then you may not be able to create the perfect quintessential look that a bride wants for her special day.” Keeping the above in mind , Mily makes sure that every bride gets a personalised experience while getting ready from her.

She believes that makeup empowers and instils confidence in self. For her makeup is not something that should make you look like a different person but a better version of yourself by accentuating your features.
